Banana Bread

A lockdown favourite - who didn't make banana bread during those long months?

This recipe can be made even better with the addition of cinnamon (if you have it!)

To make your house smell like a banana bread factory, you will need:

  • 3 over-ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 500g flour
  • 100g sugar
  • 200ml milk

Instructions

  1. Mash the bananas and stir into the flour
  2. Whisk the eggs and add into the banana flour mixture
  3. Slowly add the milk until it is all mixed in
  4. Fold in the sugar
  5. Put in the oven at 200*C for 40 minutes, or until it's looking brown on top
