Cottage Pie

One of England's most well loved and well know recipes, cottage pie is a fantastic meal that you can save the next day

You can even make it extremely cheaply using frozen vegetables and tinned potatoes

To make this recipe, you will need:

  • Beef mince
  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • Gravy granules
  • 300ml boiling water
  • Tinned potatoes
  • 30ml milk
  • 20g butter

Instructions

  1. Brown the mince
  2. Cook the frozen vegtables in the same pan
  3. Start boiling the potatoes in a seperate pan
  4. When the potatoes have boiled for 5 minutes, drain and mash. Add the milk and butter, and mix it in.
  5. Whent he mince and vegetables are cooked, add the gravy granules to the boiling water, stir in and pour into the mince pan
  6. When the gravy has thickened, transfer the mince, gravy and vegetable mixture into a deep pan
  7. Add the mashed potatoes on top and spread with a fork
  8. Cook for 20 minutes until the top is crispy
