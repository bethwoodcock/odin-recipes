Beans on Toast

What? Beans on toast?! That's not a recipe! Actually, it is!

Have you ever had 'cowboy beans'? If you're craving something a bit saucier and a kick, add a tablespoon of bbq sauce to your bean mixture

To fail at cooking, you will need:

  • A tin of beans
  • 2 pieces of bread
  • 5g of butter
  • Salt (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Cook the beans on the hob until boiling
  2. Put the pieces of toast in a toaster until browned
  3. Place the toast on a plate and spread on the butter
  4. Top with beans and a sprinkling of salt
